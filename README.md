# 🏀 SPACING — 3D Basketball E-Commerce Experience

> **Where premium basketball culture meets immersive 3D web technology.**

**SPACING** is an interactive single-page e-commerce experience for a premium basketball brand. Built with modern web technologies, it combines real-time **3D product visualization**, cinematic **scroll-driven animations**, interactive **product customization**, a futuristic **technical HUD**, and a seamless **shopping cart experience**.

---

## ✨ Key Features

* 🏀 **Interactive 3D Basketball** — Real-time WebGL rendering with realistic textures, dynamic lighting, and 360° rotation.
* 🎬 **Cinematic Hero Experience** — A 3D basketball dynamically integrated into massive `SPACING` typography.
* 📊 **Technical HUD** — Animated crosshairs and performance metrics showcasing microfiber quality, pebble depth, drag coefficient, and rotational stability.
* 🚀 **DEFY GRAVITY Section** — Bold typography, floating geometric shards, and immersive parallax effects.
* 🎨 **Customization Lab** — Instantly customize basketball colors and channel patterns such as `CLASSIC`, `STREET`, `TECH`, and `CROSS`.
* 🔄 **360° Product Preview** — Click and drag the ball to inspect your custom design from every angle.
* 🛒 **Animated Cart Drawer** — Add customized products to the cart with smooth transitions, product previews, pricing, and checkout UI.
* 📱 **Responsive Design** — Optimized experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

---

## 🎞️ Animation & 3D Experience

The application uses **GSAP ScrollTrigger** to synchronize scrolling with the persistent 3D basketball.

As users navigate the experience:

1. The `SPACING` typography fades away.
2. The basketball moves and dramatically scales up.
3. A futuristic technical HUD appears around the product.
4. Product metrics and animated data indicators enter the scene.
5. Smooth `power3.out` easing creates a cinematic and premium feel.
